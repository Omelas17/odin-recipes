Chicken Katsu

Description

Chicken Katsu, a Japanese classic Home-cooked meal served with tonkatsu sauce.

    Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Eggs
  • Panko Bread Crumbs
  • Oil

    Directions

  1. Season the chicken with salt and pepper
  2. Crack open an egg. Then place the egg, flour, and panko crumbs in separate bowls
  3. Coat chicken breast in flour, shake off excess. Proceed to dip it into the egss and press into panko crumbs
  4. Heat oil over medium-high heat
  5. Place chicken in hot oil and cook both sides for 3 to 4 mins or until golden brown
