Cajun Chicken

Description

Cajun Chicken has an earthy, savory, spicy marinated chicken recipe infused with warm spices of Cajun cuisine

    Ingredients

  • Vegetable Oil
  • Cajun Seasoning
  • Italian Style Seasoning
  • Garlic Powder
  • Lemon Pepper
  • Boneless Chicken Breast Halves

    Directions

  1. In a bowl. Mix the oil, Cajun seasoning, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, and lemon pepper
  2. Place the chicken in the bowl, turn to coat in mixture. Cover and let marinate for 1/2hour in the fridge
  3. Preheat the grill in high heat
  4. Light oil the grill, place chicken on hot grill
  5. Cook in the grill for 6 to 8 mins on each side
